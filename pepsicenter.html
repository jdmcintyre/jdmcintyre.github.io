Welcome to the Pepsi Center - Home of the Denver Nuggets

The Pepsi Center is one of the finest playing venues that the world of sports has to offer. There are over 250 events at the Pepsi Center every year. Construction of the Pepsi Center started November 20, 1997 and officially opened on October 1, 1999.

The Pepsi Center cost approximately $180 million in private funds to construct. The Pepsi Center takes up 45 acres of Downtown Denver, Colorado.

For Denver Nuggets games, the center is capable of seating 19,520 fans. The cost of the basketball court is $105,000.00. 233 pieces make up the court.

Visit the Denver Nuggets at the Pepsi Center! Located at 1000 Chopper Circle, Denver, CO 80204.
