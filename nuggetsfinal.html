Nuggets History

The Denver Nuggets organization was founded in 1967 as a charter franchise of the American Basketball Association (ABA). The team began as the Denver Larks, but then later changed their name to the Denver Rockets before the season had commenced. The team received the "Nuggets" name in 1972.
